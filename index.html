About us

Far, Fast, Life-saving

At Haast Autonomous, we build high-speed, long-range unmanned aircraft to transform how life-saving medical supplies are delivered, starting with organs. Our purpose-built autonomous hybrid VTOL aircraft replace slow, fragmented, and high-risk transport methods, dramatically reducing handoffs, delays, and cost. Our mission is to define the future of medical logistics by delivering critical cargo faster, safer, and more reliably than ever before.

The Problem

Healthcare Logistics is broken

The Organ Transport Crisis

Nearly half of all viable organs are lost each year due to avoidable transport delays. Hospitals depend on slow ground routes or costly charter flights, wasting precious minutes. For hearts and lungs, even a short delay can mean the difference between life and loss.

Explore More
Outdated, Fragmented Logistics

Organ transport relies on multiple handoffs between couriers, airlines, and medical teams. This fragmented chain creates delays, miscommunication, and zero real-time visibility. There is no centralized system to ensure speed, control, and reliability.

Explore More
Limited Access for Rural Patients

Patients in rural and remote regions face fewer transplant opportunities because of geographic limitations. Smaller hospitals frequently lack access to fast, reliable transport, leading to unequal outcomes where survival depends on location rather than medical need.

Explore More
Speed Without Friction

What the system lacks is fast, safe, and direct delivery without logistical friction. Haast Autonomous solves this with unmanned aerial transport designed for long range, precision, and reliabilityconnecting donor to recipient without delay.
