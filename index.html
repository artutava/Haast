About us

Far, Fast, Life-saving

At Haast Autonomous, we build high-speed, long-range unmanned aircraft to transform how life-saving medical supplies are delivered, starting with organs. Our purpose-built autonomous hybrid VTOL aircraft replace slow, fragmented, and high-risk transport methods, dramatically reducing handoffs, delays, and cost. Our mission is to define the future of medical logistics by delivering critical cargo faster, safer, and more reliably than ever before.
